# Human Development Society (جمعية البناء البشري)
## Organization Summary Document

---

## About the Organization

The Human Development Society (HCS) is a public benefit organization officially registered with Kuwait's Ministry of Affairs under ministerial decision **155/أ** for 2022. The organization specializes in training, workforce development, and comprehensive support services for vulnerable populations in Kuwait.

---

## Vision

> **"Leadership and excellence in serving social care groups and the general public."**

The organization strives to be a leading institution in providing quality services and support to those who need it most in Kuwaiti society.

---

## Mission

To develop the capabilities of youth from vulnerable populations through:
- Delivering quality training programs
- Promoting community development culture
- Maintaining high quality standards
- Creating employment opportunities across government sectors

## Key Projects & Initiatives

### 1. National Initiative for Employment Partners
A program focused on creating employment pathways and partnerships to help vulnerable populations enter the workforce.

### 2. National Initiative "Sanedhom" (Support Program)
A comprehensive support initiative designed to provide assistance to those in need within the community.

### 3. Recovery Initiative
A program aimed at helping individuals recover and rehabilitate, likely focusing on various aspects of personal and social development.

### 4. Hope Initiative (Aml)
An initiative centered on providing hope and opportunities for a better future to beneficiaries.

---

## Organizational Units

The society operates through several specialized units:

1. **Training Curriculum Development** - Designing and implementing effective training programs
2. **Psychological, Social, and Academic Support** - Holistic support services for beneficiaries
3. **Quality of Life Measurement and Assessment** - Evaluating and improving outcomes for social care groups
4. **International Partnerships and Knowledge Exchange** - Building global connections and sharing best practices
